## Step 3: Get DKIM Key
1. Click on business@cryptorafts.com
2. Find "DKIM" section
3. Click "Show" or "Copy" to reveal DKIM key
4. Copy the ENTIRE key (it's very long)
5. Use this key in DNS Record 4 (see DNS_RECORDS_TO_ADD.txt)
